# Compare Launch Venues

Compare the most appropriate Solana token launch venues based on the project's goals.

## Purpose

Help founders choose the best launch venue by comparing trade-offs instead of recommending a protocol immediately.

Always ask follow-up questions before making a recommendation.

---

# Information To Collect

Before comparing launch venues, ask:

1. What type of token are you launching?
   - Meme
   - Utility
   - Governance
   - DeFi
   - Gaming

2. What is your primary objective?

Examples:

- Viral community growth
- Long-term protocol
- DAO governance
- Yield generation
- Fundraising

3. Estimated launch budget

4. Expected community size

5. Do you already have liquidity?

6. Do you want concentrated liquidity?

7. Is simplicity or flexibility more important?

---

# Comparison Framework

Evaluate every venue using these categories.

- Ease of Launch
- Liquidity Model
- Customization
- Community Discovery
- Long-Term Flexibility
- Technical Complexity
- Typical Project Fit

---

# Venue Comparison

## pump.fun

Best For

- Meme coins
- Community-first launches
- Fast deployment

Strengths

- Fastest launch process
- Strong community discovery
- Low setup complexity

Trade-offs

- Limited customization
- Less suitable for complex tokenomics
- Migration required for larger ecosystems

---

## Raydium

Best For

- Utility tokens
- Governance tokens
- Protocol launches

Strengths

- Mature ecosystem
- Flexible liquidity pools
- Widely recognized

Trade-offs

- Requires liquidity planning
- More setup than pump.fun

---

## Meteora

Best For

- DeFi protocols
- Dynamic liquidity
- Capital efficiency

Strengths

- Advanced liquidity management
- Dynamic liquidity models
- Optimized capital utilization

Trade-offs

- Higher learning curve
- More complex strategy decisions

---

## Orca

Best For

- Concentrated Liquidity
- Professional market making
- Efficient LP management

Strengths

- CLMM support
- Efficient capital usage
- Strong trading experience

Trade-offs

- More advanced setup
- Requires active liquidity management

---

# Recommendation Logic

Recommend the venue that best matches the project's goals.

Do not recommend based only on popularity.

Explain why the recommended venue is the best fit.

Explain why the alternatives are less appropriate.
