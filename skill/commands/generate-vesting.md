# Generate Vesting Plan

Design a sustainable vesting schedule for a Solana token project.

## Purpose

Help founders design fair, transparent, and sustainable vesting schedules that align long-term incentives between the team, investors, advisors, contributors, and community.

Never recommend immediate unlocks for long-term stakeholders without clearly explaining the associated risks.

Always balance founder incentives with community trust.

# Vesting Review

Evaluate:

## Team

Review

- Alignment with roadmap
- Long-term commitment
- Retention incentives

Questions

Should the vesting period be longer?

Should a cliff be introduced?

---

## Investors

Review

- Unlock schedule
- Sell pressure
- Market impact

Questions

Could unlocks create excessive volatility?

---

## Advisors

Review

- Scope of contribution
- Long-term involvement

---

## Treasury

Review

- Reserve strategy
- Governance
- Sustainability

---

## Community Rewards

Review

- Incentive distribution
- Farming rewards
- Ecosystem grants

---

# Cliff Analysis

Determine whether a cliff is appropriate.

Explain:

Advantages

Disadvantages

Potential risks

---

# Unlock Strategy

Review:

- Linear vesting

- Monthly unlocks

- Quarterly unlocks

- Milestone-based unlocks

Explain the trade-offs of each approach.

---

# Risk Detection

Warn if any of the following are detected:

- No vesting

- Immediate investor unlocks

- Immediate team unlocks

- Excessive insider ownership

- Treasury unlocked immediately

- Community receives too little allocation

- Short vesting without justification

Always explain why these are risks.

---

# Best Practice Checklist

Review whether the project includes:

✓ Team vesting

✓ Investor vesting

✓ Treasury governance

✓ Transparent unlock schedule

✓ Community incentives

✓ Clear communication
