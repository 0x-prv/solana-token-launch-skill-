# Generate Tokenomics

Design a sustainable Solana tokenomics model based on the project's goals.

## Purpose

Help founders build balanced tokenomics instead of copying existing projects.

Always optimize for long-term sustainability rather than short-term hype.

Never recommend allocations without understanding the project's objectives.

---

# Tokenomics Framework

Evaluate every allocation.

## Team

Evaluate

- Team size
- Vesting
- Long-term incentives

Questions

Should the allocation be reduced?

Should vesting be extended?

---

## Community

Evaluate

- Rewards
- Incentives
- Ecosystem growth

Questions

Is enough supply reserved for users?

---

## Treasury

Evaluate

- Long-term runway
- DAO governance
- Strategic reserves

---

## Investors

Evaluate

- Vesting
- Unlock schedule
- Sell pressure

---

## Liquidity

Evaluate

- Initial liquidity
- Future liquidity incentives

---

# Sustainability Review

Evaluate

- Inflation

- Token utility

- Demand drivers

- Supply pressure

- Unlock pressure

- Long-term incentives

Identify weak areas before recommending allocations.

---

# Red Flag Detection

Warn if any of these are detected.

- Team allocation is excessively high

- No treasury allocation

- No community allocation

- Unlimited inflation

- Immediate investor unlocks

- Immediate team unlocks

- Weak token utility

- Unsustainable emissions

Always explain why each issue is risky.

---

# Suggested Allocation Framework

The exact percentages should depend on the project.

Instead of fixed numbers, explain the trade-offs between larger and smaller allocations for:

- Team
- Community
- Treasury
- Investors
- Liquidity
- Ecosystem incentives
- Advisors
- Foundation

Avoid presenting any allocation as a universal standard.
