# Launch Readiness Framework

This framework evaluates whether a Solana token project is ready for launch.

The objective is not to predict success, but to identify technical, operational, and strategic risks before deployment.

---

# Evaluation Workflow

Follow these steps in order.

1. Understand the project.
2. Determine the token type.
3. Determine the current launch stage.
4. Evaluate every launch category.
5. Assign a score for each category.
6. Identify critical blockers.
7. Recommend improvements.
8. Generate a Launch Readiness Report.

Always prioritize launch blockers before optimizations.

---

# Information To Collect

Before evaluating the project, gather:

- Token type
- Project description
- Target users
- Launch venue
- Token supply
- Allocation breakdown
- Vesting schedule
- Initial liquidity
- Treasury model
- Governance model
- Current development stage

If information is missing, ask follow-up questions before scoring.

# Evaluation Categories

## 1. Tokenomics

Evaluate:

- Supply model
- Allocation fairness
- Circulating supply
- Utility
- Inflation
- Emissions

Excellent

- Sustainable emissions
- Clear utility
- Balanced allocation

Warning Signs

- Unlimited inflation
- Weak utility
- Excessive insider allocation

## 3. Liquidity

Evaluate

- Initial liquidity
- LP ownership
- LP lock
- Market depth

Excellent

- Healthy initial liquidity
- Locked LP

Warning

- LP unlocked
- Extremely small liquidity
- Poor slippage

---

## 4. Vesting

Evaluate

- Team vesting
- Investor vesting
- Treasury unlocks

Excellent

24–48 month vesting

6–12 month cliff

Warning

Immediate unlocks

No vesting

Large insider unlocks

---

## 5. Security

Evaluate

- Audit
- Mint Authority
- Freeze Authority
- Upgrade Authority
- Multisig

Excellent

- Authorities revoked when appropriate
- Treasury controlled by multisig
- Security review completed

Warning

- Active mint authority without justification
- Treasury controlled by one wallet
- No recovery process

---

## 6. Treasury

Evaluate

- Treasury allocation
- Governance
- Spending policy
- Reserve

Excellent

- Long-term reserve
- Transparent governance

Warning

- No treasury strategy
- Centralized control

---

## 7. Community

Evaluate

- Organic users
- Documentation
- Discord
- X
- Roadmap
- Communication plan

Excellent

- Active community
- Clear roadmap
- Regular updates

Warning

- Artificial engagement
- No roadmap
- No communication strategy

---

## 8. Legal

Evaluate

- Public token sale
- Jurisdiction
- Risk disclosures

Always remind the user that legal requirements vary by jurisdiction.

Never provide legal advice.

# Critical Issue Detection

Treat these as launch blockers.

- No liquidity plan
- No LP lock
- No vesting
- Unlimited mint authority
- Treasury controlled by one wallet
- No multisig
- No devnet testing
- No incident response plan

Always explain why each issue matters.

---

# Decision Framework

When multiple valid choices exist:

1. Explain each option.
2. Compare trade-offs.
3. Recommend the best fit based on the user's goals.
4. Explain why alternatives may be less suitable.

Avoid one-size-fits-all recommendations.
