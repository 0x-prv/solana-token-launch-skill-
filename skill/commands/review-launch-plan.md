# Review Launch Plan

Review an existing Solana token launch strategy and determine whether the project is ready for launch.

## Purpose

Act as an independent launch reviewer.

Your objective is to identify launch blockers, explain risks, and recommend practical improvements before mainnet deployment.

Do not assume the project is ready.

Look for weaknesses before highlighting strengths.

---

# Information To Collect

If information is missing, ask follow-up questions before reviewing.

Collect:

- Project name
- Token type
- Project description
- Launch venue
- Total supply
- Allocation breakdown
- Vesting schedule
- Initial liquidity
- LP lock status
- Treasury model
- Governance model
- Security review status
- Current launch stage

---

# Review Workflow

Follow this order.

## Step 1

Understand the project.

---

## Step 2

Determine the token type.

Examples

- Meme
- Utility
- Governance
- Gaming
- Infrastructure
- DeFi

---

## Step 3

Determine launch stage.

Examples

- Planning
- Token Design
- Launch Preparation
- Launch Day
- Post Launch

---

## Step 4

Evaluate every category.

- Tokenomics
- Liquidity
- Vesting
- Security
- Treasury
- Community
- Legal
- Launch Strategy

---

## Step 5

Assign scores.

Each category receives a score from 0–100.

Scores should reflect the current state of the project, not future intentions.

---

## Step 6

Identify Critical Issues.

Critical Issues are launch blockers.

Examples

- No liquidity plan
- No LP lock
- Unlimited mint authority
- No multisig
- No vesting
- Weak treasury governance

---

## Step 7

Recommend improvements.

Recommendations should be ordered by impact.

Highest-risk issues first.

# Readiness Levels

90–100

Ready

The project appears well prepared.

Minor improvements only.

---

80–89

Nearly Ready

Resolve remaining issues before launch.

---

70–79

Moderate Risk

Several important improvements should be completed first.

---

Below 70

High Risk

Launching is not recommended until major issues are resolved.

---

# Red Flag Detection

Immediately flag:

- No vesting
- No liquidity strategy
- LP unlocked
- Unlimited mint authority
- Freeze authority still active without justification
- Treasury controlled by one wallet
- No governance
- No security review
- No communication plan
- No devnet testing

Explain why each issue matters.

---

# Positive Signals

Recognize good practices such as:

- Sustainable tokenomics
- Transparent allocations
- Healthy treasury
- Multisig governance
- Long-term vesting
- Locked liquidity
- Community incentives
- Security review completed
